CANDY BLAST - SOUND ASSETS
=========================

This folder is for optional sound effects and background music.

To add sounds to your game, place the following .mp3 files in this directory:

Required files:
- bgm.mp3        (Background music - looping)
- swap.mp3       (Candy swap sound)
- match.mp3      (Match made sound)
- combo.mp3      (Combo multiplier sound)
- explosion.mp3  (Large match/special candy sound)
- win.mp3        (Level complete sound)

The game will work perfectly without these files - they are optional enhancements.

Where to find free game sounds:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://opengameart.org/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

Make sure any sounds you use are royalty-free or properly licensed!
